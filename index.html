About Poornima Aarohan

Poornima Aarohan, the annual event presented by the Poornima Group of Colleges, is a celebration themed "Fictional Spectra: The World of Animated Characters." It promises a lively blend of innovation and culture, offering an uplifting experience to all attendees.

Seedhe Maut

Seedhe Maut is a hip hop duo from New Delhi, India, consisting of Siddhant Sharma and Abhijay Negi. The two met at Spit Dope Inc., a Delhi underground battle rap league organised by MC Kode and Encore ABJ. They released their first track, Seedhe Maut Anthem in May 2017, followed by their debut mixtape 2 Ka Pahada.
